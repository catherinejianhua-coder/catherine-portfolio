0-to-1 platform build · B2B2C SaaS · Baidu

Live-Commerce Operating Platform

The platform merchants and creators ran their entire live-commerce business on — products, live content, talent–merchant collaboration, marketing, campaigns and data in one place. I built it 0-to-1 (comparable in functionality to TikTok Shop's merchant & creator platform).

  • 0-to-1 platform
  • Merchant & creator tools
  • Live commerce

Decision

Merchants ran their business across a scatter of disconnected tools — slow to operate, hard to learn, with serious gaps between them. As product lead I unified products, live content, talent–merchant matching, marketing and data onto a single operating surface — so a seller could run their whole live-commerce business in one place instead of stitching it together.

Result

Collapsed broadcaster onboarding from 72 hours to under a minute and grew monthly active merchants +116% — the console became the daily operating surface sellers returned to.

Growth + new-format 0-to-1 · Huajiao

Livestream Platform Growth & New Formats

Owned user growth and the creator ecosystem on a top live-streaming platform — and shipped new 0-to-1 formats, including the company's first digital-human livestreaming and an audio live product.

  • Growth
  • Creator tooling
  • 0-to-1 formats

Decision

Treated growth as a product problem, not a spend one. Rebuilt discovery — Channels, Nearby and Moments — with AI-based content distribution so the right viewer reached the right room in fewer steps, and gave creators tooling that made them measurably more effective. On a live platform, retained creators are what retain the audience.

Result

CTR +27%, retention +10.3%, onboarding conversion +7–11%; creator tooling used across 13K+ daily livestreams.

Conversion, experimentation & A/B · Baidu

Conversion & Growth

Growth wasn't one big idea — it was finding exactly where buyers dropped off and running disciplined experiments there, instead of shipping features and hoping.

  • Funnel analysis
  • A/B experimentation
  • Targeting

Decision

Segmented the B2C purchase funnel, quantified each drop-off, and attacked the highest-leverage leak first.

Result

+12.5% conversion and +6.8% GMV on a platform at £2M+ daily GMV, delivered through 12 A/B tests over six weeks — attributable and repeatable.

Applied AI in production · Baidu

Digital Human Live Commerce

The bottleneck in live commerce was never content — it was the cost and availability of a human anchor. The win wasn't a lifelike avatar; it was an unmanned live room a merchant could trust to run 24/7.

Concept prototype · Constraint-based recommendation

FitCue

A lightweight video-matching product that helps users find feasible workouts based on today's time, space, equipment and physical constraints — not generic recommendations.

  • JTBD
  • AI matching
  • MVP scoping
  • Commercial judgement

Context

Users fail to start because they cannot tell which workout is realistic today. Broad catalogues increase choice without increasing action.

Decision

Narrowed recommendations into constraint-first feasibility matching. Each result answers "can I actually do this right now?" before "is this popular?"

Current stage

Discovery pointed to travellers, new mothers, small-space users and restarters as the sharpest-need segments — underserved by one-size-fits-all fitness products. Next step is live validation with a real cohort.
