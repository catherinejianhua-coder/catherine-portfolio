Product walkthrough

A multimodal pipeline for child-safe interactive storybooks.

AI Storybook Generator turns parent intent, age range, values and visual direction into a repeatable story-image-publishing workflow. The product challenge is not a single prompt. It is a controlled pipeline.

The pipeline adapts to different languages while the core story generation logic stays the same — language is a surface layer, not a separate product.

Example parent intent

Create a short picture book for a 1- to 3-year-old child about recognising happy and sad faces. The story should be warm, simple, safe, visually clear and suitable for reading with a parent.
Example story output Today, Gaga makes a happy face. Her cheeks go round. Her eyes shine bright.
Then Gaga makes a sad face. Her mouth turns down. She wants a cuddle.
Mum says, "Both faces are okay. Happy face, sad face, I am here with you."

Problem

Parents want stories that are age-appropriate and aligned with the values they want to teach, but generic content generation can be unsafe, inconsistent or too abstract.

Product move

Design a workflow that turns intent into structure, child-safe copy, page-level image prompts, generated assets and multiple HTML reading modes.

Validation status

The project has produced 15 interactive picture books, a generator interface and commercialisation analysis. Next proof: parent testing and willingness to pay.

Generation pipeline

1. IntentParent goal, age range, theme, value and story boundary.
2. StorySimple plot, characters, emotional arc and child-safe language.
3. VisualsPage-level image prompts and consistent illustration direction.
4. PublishInteractive HTML storybook with standard, landscape and immersive modes.
