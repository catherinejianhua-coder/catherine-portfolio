Product walkthrough

Cherish turns emotional friction into sendable words.

Cherish is an AI communication coach for fragile relationship moments. The product does not diagnose the relationship or tell the user what to do. It helps the user express what they mean in a message they can actually send.

This English surface shows the intended product journey: capture context, ask one useful clarification, identify the user's intent and return editable messages.

Example conversation

User input We haven't spoken properly for three days. The argument was small, but now I don't know how to restart without sounding like I'm giving in.
Cherish clarification What would help most right now: easing the silence, explaining what hurt, setting a boundary, or asking for an apology?
User intent I want to ease the silence first. I don't want another fight tonight.

Problem

Relationship conflict often gets stuck at the first message. Users do not need a long analysis in that moment. They need words that are clear, safe and emotionally honest.

Product move

Turn a generic chatbot into a focused workflow: context capture, one useful follow-up question, intent mapping, 2-3 sendable options and light reasoning.

Quality metric

The behavioural metric is not "AI output length". It is whether the user copies, edits, sends or continues the conversation.

Generated options

Repair-first I don't want us to keep proving who can stay silent longer. I care about us more than winning the argument. Could we pause the blame and talk for ten minutes?
Gentler I miss feeling close to you. I know we were both upset, but I don't want one argument to take over the whole week. Can we start again?
Boundary-aware I want to talk, but I also want us to do it without attacking each other. If you're open to it, I'd like to reset and explain what hurt me.
