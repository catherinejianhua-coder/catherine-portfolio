Product walkthrough

A narrative simulation for life choices with emotional consequences.

Parallel Life is framed as a serious narrative game, not an advice tool. It lets users explore possible futures, trade-offs and regrets through playable story branches.

Example choice

You have a stable role, a predictable salary and a growing sense that you are waiting for something to happen.

An early-stage AI company offers a product lead role with lower salary, higher uncertainty and more ownership.

StayProtect income, avoid disruption and accept the slow cost of becoming less curious.
MoveTake the risk, learn faster and carry the emotional load of uncertainty.

Problem

High-stakes choices are not solved by more information alone. Users struggle because each path has a hidden emotional cost.

Product move

Turn decision anxiety into a playable narrative: choice, branch, timeline, consequence and reflection.

Positioning

Keep trust pressure low by presenting the product as simulation and reflection, not prediction or therapy.

Branch preview

Three months later

The safe path feels calmer, but the same question returns during every quiet commute.

One year later

The risky path is not glamorous. It is faster, messier and more revealing than expected.

Reflection

The output is not "which choice is correct". It is a sharper view of what each choice asks the user to carry.
